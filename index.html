04 — Tracking

Email Tracking System

Invisible 1×1 pixel embedded in every email that fires a webhook the moment a recipient opens it.

Pixel Implementation
<!-- Hidden tracking pixel --> <img src="https://server/webhook/ email-open?id=trk_xxxxx" width="1" height="1" style="display:none" /> // TrackingID format: trk_{timestamp}_{contactId}_{rand}
🛡️ Anti-false-positive logic:
Opens within 2 minutes of send are ignored (sender preview filter). Opens within 30 seconds of last open are ignored (duplicate filter).

06 — Safety

Automation Safety Controls

Built-in guardrails that prevent data corruption, duplicate sends, and false analytics.

🚫
Invalid Email Filtering
Emails flagged as invalid in Google Sheets are skipped before send to protect sender reputation.
⛔
Duplicate Email Prevention
The system checks existing records before sending to ensure no contact receives the same email twice.
🕐
Sender Preview Detection
Opens within 2 minutes of send time are ignored — typically caused by the sender's own email client preview.
🔁
Duplicate Open Detection
Consecutive opens within 30 seconds of the previous open are filtered to prevent inflated open counts.
